About

Cygnet Lawn and Maintenance has been an established business since 2008. Over this time our expertise in all aspects of property maintenance and garden care has been finely honed to ensure a complete service will be provided to you to meet all your needs and desires whilst staying within your budget. Our qualified staff have 20 years experience between them and their ethical approach to garden care will ensure your garden is kept looking healthy and beautiful with minimal fuss.

We are proud local professionals who are happy to come to you. The areas we cover include:

  • Huon and Channel
  • Cygnet
  • Cradoc
  • Wattle Grove
  • Huonville
  • Kingston
  • Glen Huon
  • Geeveston
